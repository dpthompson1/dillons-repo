Rescue Walter White is a nice website about the show 'Breaking Bad'

done by Dillon Thompson

(This site is not to be confused with http://www.savewalterwhite.com/)

Breaking Bad is a show surrounding a teacher-turned-meth dealer Walter White and previous student of his, Jesse Pinkman, and their uprise to drug overlords. After being diagnosed with terminal cancer, Walter tries to make enough money to leave his wife, disabled son, and young daughter; hiding all of this from them. The show is one of the top highest aclaimed on television, with most episodes ranking higher than a seven on IMDB.

Fun fact: Did you know that the infamous pizza scene being thrown on a roof was done in one take? Crazy!

Another fact: Walter is actually Hal from Malcom in the Middle! In an extra scene included with a Breaking Bad CD collection, it reveals it was all a dream..
